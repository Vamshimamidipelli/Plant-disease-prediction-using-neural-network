About Plant Disease Detection

Our project leverages advanced neural network algorithms to provide real-time plant disease prediction. By analyzing high-resolution images of plants, the system can accurately detect early signs of diseases, enabling timely intervention to prevent crop damage and loss.

Features:

  • Real-time disease detection using AI-powered image analysis.
  • High accuracy in identifying common plant diseases.
  • Customizable recommendations for treatment and prevention.

With our innovative approach, farmers and agricultural experts can ensure healthier crops and higher yields.
